How Stats Are Calculated

All numbers are derived from competitive ArkhamDB decks

Overview Cards

Total Decks
Count of competitive decks in the analysis window (last 365 days) that used this investigator.
Optimization
(staple_count / 10 + deck_size_consistency) / 2
Measures how consistently decks include high-confidence staple cards and how stable deck sizes are. 1.0 = maximally optimized; below 0.6 = high optimization opportunity.
Diversity
unique_deck_count / total_decks
How varied deck lists are. 1.0 means every deck is completely different; 0.1 means most decks share the same card list.
Innovation
unique_cards / (avg_deck_size × total_decks) × 10
Compares the actual card variety used to the maximum possible variety if every deck ran different cards. Below 0.3 = low innovation (most decks are copies of each other).

Deck Composition

Average / Most Common Size
Mean and mode of total card counts across all decks, excluding signature cards.
Size Consistency
1 − (std_dev / mean) of deck sizes (inverted coefficient of variation).
100% = everyone builds the same size; lower = high variance in how many cards people include.

Top Cards & Staple Cards

Usage Rate
decks_with_card / total_decks
How many decks include this card. Signature cards and unique weaknesses are excluded.
Consistency Score
1 − (std_dev / mean) of the quantity run per deck.
100% = everyone runs the same number of copies; lower = variable copy counts.
Staple Confidence
usage_rate × consistency_score (capped at 1.0)
Combined signal that a card is truly essential — high only when both usage and copy-count consistency are high. Cards above 60% usage rate qualify as staples.

Rising & Falling Cards

Change Rate
(new_usage_rate − old_usage_rate) / old_usage_rate
Decks are split chronologically into two halves. Cards with >20% relative change appear here. A card at 20% usage that jumps to 30% shows +50% change.

Common Build Patterns

Pattern Detection
Each deck's top 5 highest-quantity cards form its "signature". Decks sharing the same signature are grouped into a pattern. Patterns with at least 2 decks are shown, sorted by frequency.

Hidden Gems

Gem Potential
consistency_score / (usage_rate + 0.1)
Cards run in <20% of decks but with >80% copy-count consistency. High potential = card is very consistently run at a fixed count whenever it appears — suggesting it's more effective than its low adoption implies.

Build Recommendations

Must Include
Signature cards and unique weaknesses from the investigator's deck requirements. Replacement options (e.g. parallel versions) are shown as alternatives.
Core Cards
Top staple cards by confidence score — the cards most consistently found in optimized decks for this investigator.
Hidden Gems
Underused cards with high copy-count consistency — worth testing if you want to experiment beyond the established meta.
Trending Picks
Cards with the strongest rising usage trend in recent decks.
Optimization Priority
High when optimization score < 0.6 — indicates staple cards are inconsistently included and tightening the pool would have a noticeable impact.
Innovation Opportunity
High when innovation score < 0.3 — most decks run nearly identical cards, leaving significant room to explore off-meta picks.

Meta Share

Formula
investigator_decks / all_decks_analyzed
What fraction of all competitive decks in the period belong to this investigator.

What is the Arkham Archives?

Not a deck builder. Not a collection tracker. The Arkham Archives is a reading tool — a way to understand the patterns behind investigator choices, the mathematics behind the chaos bag, and the threat profile of every scenario across every campaign.

It draws on published deck data, official card text, and game rules to surface what the numbers actually say. What cards do Agnes players keep reaching for? Which investigator survives a Carcosa bag at Expert? How dangerous is this scenario compared to the last one?

These are the questions it answers. No oracles. Just patterns and probability.
